Contact Wallz

We read everything. The fastest way to reach us is email.

Email uscontact@wallz.gg

What to write about

  • Account help: sign-in, email verification, or password resets.
  • Bug reports: tell us what happened and what device you were on.
  • Privacy and data requests: access, correction, or deletion of your personal data (see the Privacy Policy).
  • Reporting abuse or a player who is breaking the rules.
  • Press, feedback, or partnership ideas.

Response time

Wallz is run by a small team, so replies aren't instant, but we do read every message and aim to get back to you within a few days.

Managing your account yourself

You don't need to email us to leave. You can delete your account at any time from your Profile page, under the Danger Zone. For what deletion removes and retains, see the Privacy Policy.
