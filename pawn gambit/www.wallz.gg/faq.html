FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about playing Wallz, accounts, and how ranked works.

Is Wallz free?

Yes, Wallz is completely free to play. You can jump into a casual game or play the computer without spending anything or even making an account.

Do I need an account?

Not for casual, vs-computer, friend, or hot-seat games. You only need a free account for ranked matches, so that your Elo rating and match history can be tracked across games.

Is this Quoridor?

Wallz follows the well-known rules of the classic wall-and-pawn racing game: a 9×9 board with ten walls per player. It is an independent, original implementation and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any other product or publisher.

How does ranked and Elo work?

Ranked games adjust your Elo rating based on your opponent's rating and the result. Beating higher-rated players moves you up faster. The strongest ratings appear on the global leaderboard on the home page.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Wallz is built for touch and desktop equally: the same game, designed from the start to feel right on a small screen.

How do I play with a friend?

Choose “Play a Friend” to create a private game and share the code, or send a direct challenge to anyone on your friends list while they're online.

Is there a computer opponent?

Yes. “Play Computer” lets you set the difficulty by Elo, from a gentle introduction up to a genuinely strong opponent. It is a good way to practice the ideas in the strategy guide.

What happens if I disconnect mid-game?

You get a short window to reconnect and pick up exactly where you left off. If you don't make it back in time, the game is forfeited and your opponent is awarded the win.

How do I delete my account?

Open your Profile page and use the Danger Zone to delete your account. Deletion removes your personal data; the Privacy Policy explains exactly what is removed and what happens to your past games.

Does Wallz show ads?

Wallz may show ads to help keep the game free. Where ads run, we use a consent prompt in regions that require one, and you can change your choices at any time with the “Cookie settings” link in the footer. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I report a problem or a player?

Email contact@wallz.gg, or use the in-app report option on a player's profile. More on the contact page.
